GENERAL INFORMATION

The sixth Workshop of Vietnamese students in Poland (WVSiP-6) will be held on November 20-21, 2021. WVSiP-6 will be held as online event, simultaneously as on-site meeting in Cracow in the case if all restriction will be removed.
The workshop is jointly organized by Vietnam Association of Science and Technology in Poland, Institute of Physics, Pedagogical University of Cracow and Foundation for Supporting Integration of Vietnamese in Poland.
The main aim of the workshop has not been changed, i.e., it is a forum for young scientists to present the results obtained in the course of their studies and their scientific activities
WVSiP-6 will focus on the topics: : a) biology and medicine, b) physics and IT technology, c) other subjects of natural sciences and d) social sciences.
The program of WVSiP-6 is based on plenary lectures (45 min.) and invited talks (30 min.) given by distinguished specialists, contributed talks (15 min.) and poster session (with on-site poster presentation and/or online 3 min.-3 slide presentation.
WVSiP-6 will be also the opportunity for exchanging information and initiating collaborations. Therefore, all graduate and Ph.D. students and young scientists, not only in Poland but also in other countries, are welcome to participate and give the contribution to WVSiP-6.
To encourage the participation of all participants, no fee is required. The book of abstracts with ISBN number will be printed and delivered to the participants

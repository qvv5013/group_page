Welcome message

Dear participants of 5th Workshop of Vietnamese Students in Poland,

It is our great pleasure to announce that the fifth workshop of Vietnamese students in Poland in this year will be held from 28th Nov to 29th Nov 2020. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic and the global travel restrictions continue, the Organizing Committee decided to switch our physical meeting organized as so far to virtual one.
The main aim of the workshop has not been changed, i.e., to provide a platform for young scientists to report their activities, exchange information and to initiate collaborations. Therefore, the workshop is open not only for Vietnamese graduates, PhD and postdocs in Poland but also for all willing participants from other countries. Like the previous ones, this workshop is dedicated to the following topics: a) biology and medicine, b) physics and IT technology, c) other subjects of natural sciences and d) social sciences.
The workshop will host two keynote speakers and highlight numerous research reports and posters. All presentations will be in English. The plenary talk will be allowed 45 minutes and 15 minutes for an oral report. No time limit for poster presentation but the presenters are requested to provide their pdf files to organizers to post on the meeting website. The workshop is jointly organized by Vietnam Association of Science and Technology in Poland, Institute of Low Temperature and Structure Research, PAS, Institute of Physics, PAS, and Foundation for Supporting Integration of Vietnamese in Poland.
